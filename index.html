About Me

I am currently in the final stages of completing my PhD at the Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay, specializing in Electrical Engineering and Structural Health Monitoring (SHM).

Throughout my doctoral journey, I focused on the application of machine learning algorithms in SHM, particularly in collaboration with the Indian State Research Organization (ISRO) to develop an embedded system for data acquisition in composite sandwich structures.

My work involved designing experiments, creating datasets, and implementing novel approaches such as transfer learning-based convolutional autoencoders for efficient damage detection.
